CREATE A PROFILE

We warmly invite people of all backgrounds, educations, ages, appearances, and beliefs to create a profile and join our community of Audiences at IAMAIR (IAI). You are welcome here, and we value the diversity and support that each of you brings to our research and collective art projects. Best of all, it's completely free, and you don't need any prior experience or talent.

We highly value your privacy and won't sell your data to third-party entities. We'll only use your contact information to keep you updated and invite you to join our performances and events. If you ever wish to delete your profile, please contact us at contact@iamair.nl, and we'll promptly take care of it.

Please note that this website is currently in its first version. We're working on a new version where you'll be able to access and edit your profile. We appreciate any feedback, ideas, or questions you may have and welcome you to send them to us at contact@iamair.nl.
