INVITE AUDIENCES

If you are a cultural organization, museum, gallery, event, or art fair, IAMAIR invites you to work with us and bring our innovative approach to cultural mediation to your visitors. Our goal is to generate new forms of knowledge production in cultural institutions through interactive performances that challenge visitors' assumptions and encourage critical reflection.

Our services are tailored to your institution's goals and needs, and we are committed to creating a transformative experience that engages your visitors in a unique way. All you need to do is fill in the form below to tell us more about your goals and needs, and we will contact you soon to arrange a meeting over coffee and discuss how we can work together.

Please note that our project is an artistic research, and we are continuously working on improving our services. The data you provide in the form is kept private in our database and is only used for research purposes. We appreciate any feedback, ideas, or questions you may have and welcome you to send them to us at contact@iamair.nl.

Thank you for your interest in working with IAMAIR. We look forward to exploring the boundaries of cultural mediation together!
