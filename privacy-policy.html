PRIVACY POLICY

Effective date: 21/04/2023

Our website (www.iamair.nl) is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect personal information we receive from users of our website.

Information collection and use

We may collect personal information from users in various ways, including when users visit our website, register on the website, place an order, fill out a form, or otherwise interact with our website. The information we collect may include the user's name, email address, mailing address, phone number, or other information that users provide to us. We may use the information we collect from users to improve our website, personalize user experience, respond to user inquiries or requests, process transactions, or send periodic emails.

Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of personal information.

Children's privacy

Our website is not intended for use by children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under the age of 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

Changes to the privacy policy

We may update or modify this privacy policy from time to time, and the updated policy will be posted on our website with the new effective date.

Contact us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy or the use of personal information, please contact us at contact@iamair.nl.
