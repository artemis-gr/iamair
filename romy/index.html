Artists Borrow Romy's Voice

Were you treated unfairly by the art system?
Do you wish you could speak up, but feel like you can't?

Let Romy speak for you!

Romy Bauer, a fictional spoken word artist and real life avatar, will give voice to the realities of artists navigating the art world. If you have a story to share, a frustration, a moment of injustice, a truth you feel silenced from expressing, you can lend Romy your words. She will speak them on your behalf. You can share your story through this box; it is anonymous unless you decide to sign. No names will be disclosed in the final presentations, so if you wish to be credited, let us know.
